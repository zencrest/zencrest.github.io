## grow up

- She grew up in Hefei.
- It's time to grow up and take responsibility. 是时候成熟起来，承担责任了。
- Taking responsibility for myself is part of the process of growing up.

## hang up

- She hung up on me when Lu's call came in.因为陆打来了电话，她就挂断了我的通话。
- Please hang up your coat in the closet.请把你的外套挂在衣柜里。

## heat up

- The sun quickly heated up the car's interior. 太阳很快就把车内晒热了。heat up the soup

## hike up
- She hiked up her skirt and climbed onto the bicycle.她撩起裙子，跨上了自行车。
- He hiked up his trousers to cross the stream.他卷起裤腿蹚过小溪。
- He hiked up his sleeves and got down to work.他挽起袖子开始干活。

## hold up

- My shoes have held up well for years.这双鞋穿了好几年依旧很耐穿。
- Will this straw shed hold up in a storm?这间草棚能扛得住暴风雨吗？
- The wooden hut didn't hold up against the strong wind.
- Traffic was held up for several hours by the accident.事故导致交通堵塞了数小时。
- There was a hold-up on the highway due to an accident.
- The bad weather caused a significant hold-up in our travel plans.恶劣天气导致我们的旅行计划出现重大延误。

## hurry up

- We need to hurry up if we want to catch the train/flight.
- Can you hurry up with that report? I need it by noon.你能快点完成那份报告吗？我中午前需要它。

## hush up

- There was a financial scandal involving the Chief Risk Officer (CRO), but it was all hushed up.一桩涉及首席风控官的财务丑闻，最终被全盘掩盖了。

## keep up

- She runs so fast, I can't keep up.她跑得太快了，我跟不上。
- If you keep up practicing, you'll get better.如果你坚持练习，你会变得更好。

## lift up

- She smiled as he gently lifted her up.他轻轻地抱起她时，她笑了。
- A good song can really lift up your spirits.一首好歌真的能振奋你的精神。

## line up

- Please line up at the entrance.请在入口处排队。
- We need to line up a meeting with the client.我们需要安排与客户的会议。
