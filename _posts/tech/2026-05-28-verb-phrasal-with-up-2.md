## dig up/into

- I am trying to dig up solid evidence to take him down. Uncover hard proof to bring him down.
- They're going to dig up the road to fix the water pipes.

## draw up/into

- We need to draw up a detailed plan for the project.我们需要为这个项目制定一个详细的计划。
- We drew up a work plan. 我们拟定了一份工作计划。
- A taxi drew up outside the hotel, and Momo got out.=A taxi pulled up at the hotel, and Momo stepped out.
- A car drew up beside me. 一辆车停在了我身旁。

## dream up

- Xie dreamed up a bad idea to take her down.他想出了一个坏点子，打算扳倒她。
- dream up → come up with /cook up（cook up 更侧重 “蓄意编造”）

## dress up/in

- Are you going to dress up for the date tonight?今晚约会你打算精心打扮一番吗？get dressed up
- She tried to dress up the old house with new paint and decorations.他们试图用新油漆和装饰品美化旧房子。

## drink up/eat up

- He quickly drank up the milk.人在囧途
- Drink up! The soup is getting cold.

- I quickly ate up all the cookies.
- This old car eats up a lot of gas.这辆旧车耗费大量汽油。
- The new software will eat up a lot of memory.新软件会占用大量内存。

## dry up

- The rice fields began to dry up during the long drought.久旱无雨，稻田开始干涸龟裂。
- His ideas for the essay seemed to dry up after a few weeks.几周之后，他写文章的思路就枯竭了。
- My mind dried up and I couldn't write a single word.我脑子一片空白，一个字也写不出来。
- He suddenly dried up in the middle of the speech.他演讲到一半突然卡壳了。

## end up

- After spending two hours with her, I ended up taking a rideshare to ZJL South Station in Kunshan.
- I ate too much chicken she cooked and ended up with a stomachache.我吃了太多她做的鸡肉，最后肚子疼了。
- If you keep doing that, you'll end up in trouble.如果你继续那样做，你最终会惹上麻烦。

## fill up

- I need a big meal to really fill up.我需要大餐才能真正吃饱。
- The buffet allowed us to fill up on all our favorite dishes.自助餐让我们尽情享用所有我们喜欢的菜肴。
- I'll fill up the water bottle before I leave for home.我下班回家前会把水杯装满。
- I'll top up my water bottle before heading home.
- The hall quickly filled up with people.大厅很快就坐满了人。
