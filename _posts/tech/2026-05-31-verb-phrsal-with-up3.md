## look up

- I can look up his address online.我可以在网上查找他的地址。
- Things are finally starting to look up for us.我们的境况终于开始好转了。
- Business has been tough, but now things are looking up.之前生意不景气，如今总算有了起色。
- I'll look you up next time I'm in town.我下次进城时会拜访你。

## make up

- He made up an excuse for being late.他编了个迟到的借口。
- Make up your bed after getting up.起床后把床铺整理好。
- She spends 10 minutes making up every morning.她每天早上花十分钟化妆。make oneself up 给自己化妆
- These chapters make up the whole book.这些章节构成了整本书。
- Please make up the difference.请补足差额。
- I'll make up for lost time by working extra hours.我会通过加班来弥补失去的时间。
- I’m really sorry for keeping you waiting an extra hour.How can I make it up to you?
- I made up my mind to file a report to...

## measure up

- The new escort doesn't quite measure up to our expectations.新来的陪同人员并未达到我们的预期。
- measure up to sb's expectations/standards 达到某人的期望 / 标准
- His work doesn't measure up to the company's standards.他的工作达不到公司标准。
- This product fails to measure up to our requirements.这款产品不符合我们的要求。

## mess up

- I really messed up the presentation.我把演示搞砸了。
- Don't get your clothes messed up before the annual party.

## open up

- We need to open up more opportunities for young people.我们需要为年轻人创造更多机会。
- It took her a long time to open up about her past.她花了很长时间才敞开心扉谈论她的过去。
- She felt comfortable enough to open up to her therapist.

## pack up

- She needs to pack up her belongings before we leave.出发前她需要收拾好个人物品。
- Please pack up your desk. 请整理好你桌上的物品。
- The printer has packed up again.打印机又坏了。break down（通用，设备 / 车辆故障） give out（老化后停运）

## perk up

- A cup of black tea always helps me perk up in the morning.
- She perked up after hearing the good news.听到好消息，她一下子来了精神。
- The dog's ears perked up at the sound of the doorbell.听到门铃声，狗的耳朵竖了起来。
- I perked up, listening intently to the conversation. I brightened up and listened carefully to the talk.

## put up

- We need to put up the tent before it gets dark.天黑前我们需要搭好帐篷。
- Can you put me up for the night?今晚你能收留我吗？
- I can't put up with her constant complaining anymore.
- He put up a suggestion at the meeting.他在会上提出了一项建议。

## rack up

- I racked up a lot of debt during my time at university.我读大学期间欠下了不少债务。I ran up a lot of debt in college.
- He racked up huge credit card bills.他欠下了巨额信用卡账单。
- The team racked up five wins in a row.这支队伍接连拿下五场胜利。

## rip up

- I decided to rip up the old photographs/letters.
- They decided to rip up the contract after the dispute.他们决定在争议后撕毁合同。

## save up

- I'm trying to save up for a new car.
- She's been saving up all year for renovating the old house.她一整年都在攒钱，准备翻新老房子。
